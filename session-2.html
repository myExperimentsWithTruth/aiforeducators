What this session covered

Grounding, and why it changes the answer

Session 1 showed that a good prompt gets you a long way. This session shows where a prompt alone cannot reach. Grounding an assistant in your own sources changes what it can honestly say, and it is the difference between material you can put your name to and material you cannot.

  • 1What grounding is, and why a prompt cannot replicate it.
  • 2Two tools, two jobs: where a chat assistant ends and NotebookLM begins.
  • 3Decomposing the work so quality holds across a long build.
  • 4Verifying a citation, every time, before you rely on it.
