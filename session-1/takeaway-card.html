The mental model

A Harvard-educated five-year-old

Brilliant and well-read, but naive about your world. It cannot guess your context — you have to give it. And it is confidently wrong: it will make the mistake and sound certain doing it. Fluent is not the same as correct.

Four parts of a good prompt
  • Context 60 students, mid-term Monday, Hall B.
  • Role Their course instructor.
  • Task Write a reminder email.
  • Format Short, with a subject line.

A prompt can grow — a saved way of working (a skill) can run to hundreds of lines and carry your whole style.

Responsible use

The principle we leave with

AI is not banned, and it is not a shortcut around thinking. The tool drafts and speeds the work; the ideas, the verification and the accountability stay with us. In a report or DPR that rides real funding, the risk is not copied text — it is an unverified, AI-generated fact riding a decision.

Verification and accountability are the controls — and both are human.

Disclose

Say when AI helped produce the work.

Verify

Check every fact, figure and citation.

Protect data

Keep IP and personal data out of public tools.

Stay accountable

The author owns the output, not the tool.
