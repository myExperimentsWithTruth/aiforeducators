What this session covered

Where AI fits, and how to prompt it

The opening session builds the shared vocabulary the rest of the programme rests on. It starts from what these tools actually are, moves to asking for what you want, and ends with an honest look at the wider landscape and the guardrails that come with it.

  • 1A working mental model of what AI is and what it is not.
  • 2Prompting with Context, Role, Task and Format.
  • 3The tool landscape, covered evenhandedly rather than sold.
  • 4Responsible use: what to share, what to verify, what you own.
